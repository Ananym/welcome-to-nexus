# Minor Quest Locations

Some creatures (like Rats) can be found in more than one location. Minor quests can be completed once per day (approx. every 22 hours) with a catch-up rate of one additional quest per 3 days of life you've missed.

**Note:** You only get minor quest experience for 1 per day. Find the location nearest you and get that quest done!
