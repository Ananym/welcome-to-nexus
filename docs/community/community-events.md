# Community Events

You may notice community events being advertised via global messages or on the [Community Events board](community/boards.md). Attend them! Community events are among the best things NexusTK has to offer when run well. These events are typically held by clans, subpaths, or ministries, and can be very rewarding if you win. They cover a range of games from casual to serious, luck to skill.

You may not be able to participate in every event, as some depend on knowing certain spells. You can always ask the host via whisper if the event is appropriate for you. Even if you can't participate, it's still fun to spectate and chat.

Unregistered players cannot enter the subpath public areas where subpath events are often held, but a host can summon you in if you ask nicely.

Be respectful of the event, the players, and the host. Don't insert yourself into the game or play area inappropriately, and don't pick up decorations or items used to mark the path to the venue.

Unless otherwise noted, times for events are usually given in EST, Eastern Standard Time, GMT-5.

Hosting your own events is a great way to contribute to your clan or subpath, but always check with leadership first!
